Social Skew Bar displays a static social sharing bar on the top of you blog.

== Description ==


There are lot of Social Plugins that help you integrate your Wordpress Blog with Social Media sites, Social Skew Bar is a new concept of Social Media Plugin. 

[Social Skew Bar](http://www.designskew.com/blog/social-skew-bar-sharing-wordpress-plugin) includes Twitter, Facebook and Google Plus integration and displays them statically on the top of your wordpress blog. It will be on the top while you scroll the page.

**Why I need Social Skew Bar plugin ?**

This plugin is helpful in many ways, it helps you spread your blog on social media sites which will help you getting traffic. It also helps to [improve seo](http://www.designskew.com/blog/wordpress-hacks-to-improve-seo-ranking/) of your blog as google considers social factors while ranking your articles.

== Installation ==

Here's how you to install the plugin:

1. Upload the plugin to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Enter your Facebook Page URL and Twitter Handler in "Your Profile" Page.
1, 2, 3: You're done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Facebook and Twitter follow buttons are not showing ? =

You need to enter your Facebook Page URL and Twitter username in "Your Profile" Page.
